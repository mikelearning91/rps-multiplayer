How To Play

  1. Join the game by entering your name/alias text space and click "join"
  2. Wait for another player to join. If a player is already in the game, game on!
  3. Wait till it's your turn and choose Rock, Paper or Scissors by clicking on the buttons (each labeled appropriately: "Rock", "Paper", "Scissors".
  4. Have fun & Good luck!

Tips

  • Try to recognize a pattern in your opponents choices.
  • But don't overthink this. This is a very simple game!
  • Friendly trash talk is encouraged! Use the chatbox.
